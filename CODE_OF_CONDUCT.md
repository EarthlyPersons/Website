# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free environment.

Examples of expected behavior:

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent
- welcome feedback and different viewpoints
- focus on impact and solutions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language
- personal attacks or intimidation
- sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block participation for behavior that violates this code.
